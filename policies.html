Privacy Policy

Information Collection:

1. Personal Information: When you register for a library card or use our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, address, email, phone number, and, if applicable, student or library card number.

2. Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our library services, including borrowed materials, search queries, and interactions with our website.

Use of Information:

1. Providing Services: We use the collected information to provide library services, manage accounts, process requests, and offer personalized recommendations.

2. Improvement of Services: Information gathered helps us improve our library services, website, and offerings to better serve our users.

Borrowing Books Policy

Library Membership:

1. To borrow books and access library services, a valid library card is required. Users must present their library card at the time of borrowing.

Loan Period:

1. The standard loan period for books is 1 - 3 days and its depends in thelibrary what type of books will borrow. Users may renew borrowed items unless they have been requested by another user.

Renewals:

1. Books can be renewed 1 times unless there is a hold on the item. Renewals can typically be done online, in-person, or gmail.

Restricted Items:

1. Some items, such as reference books or rare materials, may not be available for borrowing and can only be used within the library premises.

Returning Books:

Books should be returned directly to the library or designated book return locations during operating hours. Items returned after closing will be considered returned on the next open day.

Penalty Policy

Overdue Items:

1. Users are responsible for returning borrowed materials by the due date to avoid late fees. Overdue fines its depend in the books per day may apply.

Lost or Irreparably Damaged Items:

1. Users are responsible for the cost of lost or damaged items. Fees will be charged for lost or irreparably damaged books.

Payment Method:

1. Fines and fees can be paid in cash, through library's circulation desk.

Unpaid Fees:

1. Failure to pay fines or fees may result in the suspension of borrowing privileges until outstanding charges are settled.

Multiple Penalties:

1. It may result of suspension of both borrowing privileges and library account access until outstanding charges are settled.
